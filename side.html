IITU is the champions of Case4Success 2023!


10/11/2023

IITU is the champions of Case4Success 2023!

On October 6, the international case championship Case4Success 2023 was held at the University of Turan.

The case championship was attended by 11 teams from different universities, who tested their strength in solving a business case for the International Company Uniliver.

Prize-winning places went to: 
                1st place - IITU

                2nd place - KBTU

                3rd place - KIMEP
Congratulations to our students and wish them success in future championships

- NIC played a juicy-pong game.
